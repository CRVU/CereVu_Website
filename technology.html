Our Technology

Two decades of research prove pain can be objectively measured

The Science

Two decades of research demonstrate that pain can be measured using functional MRI by tracking changes in cerebral oxygen consumption (BOLD fMRI).

Real-Time Monitoring

CereVu's noninvasive optical forehead sensor uses near-infrared spectroscopy to continuously assess cerebral oxygenation changes in real-time.

Proprietary Algorithm

Our AI-driven algorithms translate changes in HbO₂ into an objective CereVu Pain Index, personalized for each patient.
