Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Date to be determined]

CereVu Medical ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how the CereVu Clinical mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and protects information when used by authorized clinical and research users.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect and process the following types of information:

  • User Information: Name, professional role, institutional affiliation, and contact information provided for account access.
  • Clinical and Research Data: De-identified or limited patient-related data collected through the CereVu system for clinical, research, and device performance purposes.
  • Device and Usage Information: App usage data, device type, operating system version, and technical logs used to maintain and improve App performance.

The App is not intended for use by the general public or children.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Enable secure access to the App for authorized users
  • Support clinical, research, and operational use of the CereVu Medical system
  • Monitor, maintain, and improve App functionality and performance
  • Comply with applicable legal, regulatory, and institutional requirements

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal information.

Information may be shared only:

  • With affiliated healthcare institutions, research partners, or service providers acting on our behalf
  • To comply with legal obligations, regulatory requirements, or lawful requests
  • In accordance with applicable healthcare and data protection regulations (e.g., HIPAA, where applicable)

4. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law, regulation, or institutional policy.

6. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials and for using the App in accordance with applicable laws, institutional policies, and professional obligations.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on our website, with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact:

CereVu Medical
Website: https://www.cerevumedical.com

Investigational Use Only
