Committed to patient care

CereVu Medical is a private company working with doctors and nurses toward the common goal of improving patient care through its AI-driven wearable sensor that enables continuous monitoring of routine vital signs (heart rate, SpO₂, respiratory rate), body activity, and coughing dynamics, along with novel biomarkers for objective assessment of pain and dyspnea (air hunger).

Investigational Use Only

CereVu's technology is investigational and has not been cleared or approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA). The technology is intended for research and evaluation purposes only and is not currently intended for clinical diagnosis, treatment, or medical decision-making.
