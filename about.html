About CereVu Medical

CereVu Medical is revolutionizing pain management with our proprietary wearable sensor technology. Building on two decades of neuroscience research, we've developed the first objective, real-time pain measurement system that transforms how healthcare providers assess and treat pain.

Our team combines expertise in AI, medical devices, and wearables to deliver clinically validated solutions. With over $2 million in NSF and FDA grants, published clinical data, and recognition in Nature magazine, we're leading the future of objective pain assessment.

Clinical Excellence

Validated through rigorous studies with 400+ patients and published peer-reviewed research.

Innovation

Patent-protected technology translating decades of fMRI research into a practical wearable solution.

Impact

Addressing a $750B healthcare challenge affecting 50+ million Americans annually.

Regulatory Progress

FDA Pre-submission meeting completed with Anesthesia/Respiratory Branch. De novo 510(k) pathway established for 300-patient clinical trial.
